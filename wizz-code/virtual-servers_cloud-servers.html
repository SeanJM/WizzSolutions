Why Cloud?

Cloud hosting is all about elastic on demand resources, you can scale up/down your CPU/memory when you need it. On demand resources means you will not have to move your data when upgrading. Cloud hosting also provides high availability and maximum uptime. Instead of having your server (VM, virtual machine) on a single physical server, your server resources will be a slice of a large cluster resource pool. Our cloud infrastructure is built on VMware vSphere.
